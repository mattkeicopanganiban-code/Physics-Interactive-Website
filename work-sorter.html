Categorize the following actions:

Is Work Being Done? (YES or NO)

1. Lifting a box straight up onto a shelf

YES - Force (against gravity) and displacement (upward) are in the same direction

2. Holding a box still above your head

NO - There is no displacement, so no work is done (even though you're using energy)

3. Pushing a car sideways while it rolls forward

NO - The force is perpendicular to the motion, so no work is done on forward movement

4. Lowering a box slowly to the ground

YES - Force and displacement are in the same direction (downward)

5. Walking carrying a heavy briefcase

YES - Force is pushing you forward, and displacement is forward, so work is being done

6. A book resting on a table

NO - There is no motion or displacement

Categorize by Type of Work:

Positive, Negative, or Zero Work?

1. Friction slowing down a moving car

NEGATIVE - Friction opposes motion, so work is negative

2. A rope pulling a sled forward

POSITIVE - Force and displacement are in same direction

3. Air resistance acting on a falling ball (sideways wind)

ZERO - Air resistance perpendicular to motion does zero work on vertical motion

4. Gravity pulling a ball downward as it falls

POSITIVE - Gravity and displacement are both downward

5. You pushing against a wall that doesn't move

ZERO - No displacement means no work

6. A braking force stopping a bicycle

NEGATIVE - Brakes oppose motion direction
