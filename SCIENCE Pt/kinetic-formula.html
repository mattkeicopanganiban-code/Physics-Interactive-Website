Question 1: Kinetic Energy = ½ × m × ______²

Answer: v (velocity). The formula is KE = ½mv²

Question 2: If m = 3kg and v = 6 m/s, then KE = ______ Joules

Answer: 54. KE = ½ × 3 × (6)² = 1.5 × 36 = 54 Joules

Question 3: Rearrange to find velocity: v = √(2 × KE ÷ ______)

Answer: m (mass). From KE = ½mv², we get v = √(2KE/m)

Question 4: An object has 200 Joules of KE and mass 4kg. What is its velocity? ______ m/s

Answer: 10. v = √(2 × 200 ÷ 4) = √(400 ÷ 4) = √100 = 10 m/s

Question 6: A 2kg object at rest accelerates to 5 m/s. KE gained = ______ Joules

Answer: 25. Initial KE = 0, Final KE = ½ × 2 × (5)² = 1 × 25 = 25 Joules
