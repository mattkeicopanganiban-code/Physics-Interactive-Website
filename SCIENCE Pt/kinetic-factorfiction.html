✅ Kinetic Energy Fact or Fiction

True or False? Click to reveal the answer!

Statement 1: Kinetic energy only exists when an object is moving.

Correct! KE = ½mv². When v = 0 (stationary), KE = 0

Statement 2: Doubling the mass doubles the kinetic energy.

Correct! KE ∝ m, so doubling mass doubles KE (if velocity stays same)

Statement 3: Doubling the velocity doubles the kinetic energy.

Correct! KE ∝ v², so doubling velocity quadruples KE

Statement 4: A light fast object can have more KE than a heavy slow object.

Correct! KE depends on both m and v². A light but very fast object can exceed a slow heavy one

Statement 5: Kinetic energy can be negative.

Correct! KE = ½mv² is always positive (or zero). Both m and v² are positive

Statement 6: An object moving in a circle at constant speed has zero kinetic energy.

Correct! It has KE because it's moving (v ≠ 0). The speed is constant, so KE is constant
