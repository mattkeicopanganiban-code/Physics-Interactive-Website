Kinetic Energy Quiz - Test Your Knowledge!

Question 1: What is the formula for kinetic energy?

A) KE = mgh
B) KE = ½mv²
C) KE = Fd
D) KE = at
KE = ½mv², where m is mass and v is velocity. Kinetic energy depends on velocity squared!

Question 2: A 2kg object moves at 5 m/s. What is its kinetic energy?

A) 10 Joules
B) 20 Joules
C) 25 Joules
D) 50 Joules
KE = ½mv² = ½ × 2 × (5)² = 1 × 25 = 25 Joules

Question 3: If you double the velocity of an object, what happens to its kinetic energy?

A) It doubles
B) It triples
C) It quadruples
D) It increases by half
It quadruples! Since KE ∝ v², doubling v means KE increases by 2² = 4 times

Question 4: A stationary object has what kinetic energy?

A) The same as if it were moving
B) Undefined
C) Zero
D) Infinite
Zero! KE = ½mv² = 0 when v = 0 (object is not moving)

Question 5: A 4kg object is moving at 3 m/s. What is its kinetic energy?

A) 12 Joules
B) 18 Joules
C) 24 Joules
D) 36 Joules
KE = ½mv² = ½ × 4 × (3)² = 2 × 9 = 18 Joules
